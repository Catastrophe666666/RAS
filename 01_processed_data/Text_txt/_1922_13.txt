A PARTIAL BIBLIOGRAPHY
OF CHINESE BIRDS.
By J. H. RILEY AND Dr. C. W. RICHMOND
of the SMITHSONIAN INSTITUTION..
This partial bibliography of Chinese Birds was largely
compiled by Mr. Riley, for personal convenience, without
any attempt at bibliographical style, and supplemented by
Dr. Charles W. Richmond, who says, "This bibliography is
doubtless quite incomplete, but it contains much that
has been written on the birds of China and countries border-
ing it. Some of the titles, such as 'New Species of Bul-
buls,' do not indicate any relationships to China, but they
contain descriptions of Chinese species or discuss Chinese
species. Some of the general works, like the 'British
Museum Catalogue of Birds,' have been omitted." Mr. A.
de C. Sowerby thinks it will be of the greatest value, and so
we publish it for the benefit of our readers.-Editor.
